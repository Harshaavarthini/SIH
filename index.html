Counter Hijack - Sky Marshalling Operation

Our Counter Hijack and Sky Marshalling Simulator is designed to prepare you for the challenges of safeguarding the skies. By immersing yourself in realistic training scenarios and environments, you can develop the skills and confidence needed to respond effectively to critical incidents..

FEATURES

  • Scenario Customization: Instructors can customize scenarios to match specific training objectives and challenges faced by their organizations. This flexibility ensures that the training remains relevant and up-to-date.
  • Day and Night Modes: Customize your training experience by choosing between day and night scenarios. This feature allows trainees to practice in different lighting conditions, enhancing their adaptability to real-life situations.
  • Realistic Aircraft Models: Our simulator features a wide range of realistic aircraft models to ensure trainees are familiar with various types of commercial and private planes, helicopters, and other aircraft. This familiarity is crucial for effective decision-making during emergencies.
  • Dynamic Environments: Trainees will face diverse and dynamic environments, including different weather conditions (e.g., rain, fog, snow), airport layouts, and geographical locations. This variability ensures a well-rounded training experience.
  • Multiplayer Mode: Collaborate with fellow trainees in multiplayer mode to simulate coordinated responses to hijack and emergency situations. Teamwork and communication are essential in real-life scenarios, and our simulator reflects this reality.
